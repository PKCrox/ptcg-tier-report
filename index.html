Methodology & limitations
  • Meta Score adjusts win rate for opponent strength and determines tier placement.
  • Sample adjustment pulls low-volume decks toward 50% to reduce small-sample inflation.
  • Pilot concentration ⚠ flags variants where one team supplies 80%+ of games or fewer than three teams played the deck.
  • Rating cohorts use current leaderboard ratings because historical match-time ratings are unavailable.
